Terms and Conditions


Welcome to the Muts' Annual Trip 2024 set to be at Phillip Island.
Please take a moment to familiarise yourself with the following terms and conditions.
By participating in the voting, you agree to abide by the following rules and regulations.



Voting for Your Housemates


Each Mut is allowed to cast votes for a maximum of two fellow Muts they wish to share a house with during the trip.
There is no dedicated order to your votes. That is, your first vote has no greater weight or influence than your second vote. They are deemed equal.
Once submitted, your votes are final. If you made a mistake when submitting your votes, you must send an email to the esteemed trip organizer and creator of The Algorithm™.

We acknowledge that there will be unforeseen circumstances resulting in Muts who have placed votes and/or been voted for pulling out from the event. These cases will be handled accordingly and fairly under the Muts Fair Work Act 1979.


Personal Information


To participate, you must sign up under your own name and use a valid email address. Pseudonyms and disposable email addresses will lead to disciplinary action taken against you.
If you decide to sign up under someone else's name your votes and every vote for you will be disregarded and you will be place in a house at random once the results are complete.

Don't worry about passwords being leaked or known to the creator of this webpage. Each password is encrypted using SHA-256 and encrypted during comparison.


The Algorithm™ has the Final Say


The results, as determined by The Algorithm™, are absolutely final. There's no room for negotiations or debates.
If you do decide to dispute the results, you must do so formally, with a proper complaint submitted to the esteemed trip organizer and creator of The Algorithm™.
